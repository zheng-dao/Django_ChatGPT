Estate Planning

May 19, 2021
 
12pm
 
Join us to discuss the most common questions surrounding estate planning and why it is important to set up a plan that will protect you and your loved ones.

Topics will include the elements that make up a strong estate plan, the key differences in the most common types of estate planning documents, and how to choose a plan that is the best for your individual needs.

This workshop is free, open to everyone and you can easily stream it directly to your computer, smartphone or other device.

$90,000 Available in Student Scholarships
